Social Driven Behavior and Search








Social Media has become an important platform for people to collaborate, communicate and staying together.

There is a huge amount of unstructured data in form of comments, likes/dislikes, conversations and consumer preferences.

This data gives enormous advantage to the organization where they can understand the consumer preferences, recent trends/fads and branding/marketing related information.

The Social driven Behaviour and Search is about searching the social media (Facebooks, LinkedIn, Twitter, Google Plus) with the Direct and Behavioral search.

The search options includes having a global search and searching in the connected/closed world for a given person (id).

The System also targets creating a social linked tree of the given members, where the search can be made.
